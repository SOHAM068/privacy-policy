Privacy Policy for com.srm_facultyalarmapp

Last Updated: 27/09/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to com.srm_facultyalarmapp. This app is designed to help university faculty members set reminders for punching their attendance. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Alarm and Reminder Data: Information related to the alarms you set (e.g., time, duration).
  • Device Information: This includes data such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log Data: In the event of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party services) on your device called Log Data. This may include information like your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, device name, operating system version, and other statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Alarm Functionality: To ensure that the app can set and manage alarms based on the user's input.
  • To Improve User Experience: To analyze app usage data to improve the performance and user experience.
  • To Fix Issues: To identify and resolve any technical issues in the app.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties, except when required by law or if you explicitly consent to it.

6. Permissions Required

For the app to function properly, we require the following permissions:

  • Alarm Permission: To set alarms for the times you specify.
  • Exact Alarm Permission: To ensure alarms are triggered at the precise times, we may request permission to set exact alarms on Android devices.

These permissions are essential for the core functionality of the app and are not used for any other purposes.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for use by university faculty members and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal data from a child under 13, we will delete such information from our records.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on this page.
